Informations:

DEBS registrations include attendance to the WoAIS workshop, welcome session, social event, conference dinner, lunches for the entire duration of the event and coffee breaks.
A full or ACM Professional member registration is required for each paper accepted for publication in the proceedings. Requests to cancel the registration must be addressed directly to the local organization chair (Diogo Lima).

The social event includes a guided walking tour by Lisbon downtown and a dinner at Zambeze restaurant.

Additional tickets for the social event and conference dinner are avaliable for 90€ depending on room availability. Contact the local organization chair (Diogo Lima) in advance for reservations.

The following rules apply:

  • Requests received before Sunday, the 24th of May 23:59 AoE will receive a 50% refund of the registration fee, minus any transaction/processing fees.
  • Requests received after Sunday, the 24th of May 23:59 AoE and before Friday the 12th of June 23:59 AoE will receive a 25% refund of the registration fee, minus any transaction/processing fees.
