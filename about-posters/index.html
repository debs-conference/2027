Poster Printing Information

The poster size is A0.

If you prefer to print your poster on-site rather than bringing it with you, we point you to two services:

  • Option 1: Email Submission and On-Site Payment
    Send your poster as a PDF file to copyprint.fc.ul@staples.pt. Please indicate in the email that you want the poster to be printed in A0 size.
    Once your poster is ready, you will receive a confirmation reply. You can then collect it in person and pay at Staples in the campus.
    Important: Please send the email at least three working days before you plan to collect the poster, and CC the Poster Chairs Manisha Luthra and Bernardo Almeida.
  • Option 2: Printing store near the venue
    If you prefer you can print your poster in "CopyCenter Centro de Cópias" which prints posters on the spot.
